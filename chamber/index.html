New firefighters learn 'The Colony Way'

Welcome aboard! The Colony Fire Department recently hired seven new candidate firefighters. From Dec. 8-11, the new recruits completed a 40-hour in-service fire academy comprised of four 10-hour days. During this time they expanded on their training by working closely with veteran TCFD firefighters to learn "The Colony Way" on engine operation, truck operation, and general overall operations within the department.
